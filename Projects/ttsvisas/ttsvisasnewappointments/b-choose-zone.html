Visa applicants should make their visa applications at the Consulate with jurisdiction over the place where the applicant lives and conducts his or her life.
US Consulate Chennai (Madras) Covers residents of the southern states of Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Lakshadweep Islands and Pondicherry.
US Embassy New Delhi Covers residents of Delhi and the north Indian states of Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Punjab, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh
US Consulate Calcutta (Kolkata) Covers residents of Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Tripura, Bihar, Orissa, West Bengal, Arunachal Pradesh, Sikkim, Mizoram, Jharkhand, Andaman and Nicobar Islands
