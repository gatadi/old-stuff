Lab08b: Clearing Floats

Prerequisites, Goals, and Outcomes

Prerequisites: Before you begin this exercise, you need mastery of the following:
  • Cascade and Inheritance
  • Altering display types (i.e. inline to block)
  • Fixing IE bugs
  • Using floats to achieve horizontal alignment of list items
  • Manipulating padding and margins to control display of background colors

Goals: Reinforce your ability to solve the problem of floated elements escaping the boundaries of their parents

Outcomes: You will demonstrate mastery in the exercise by using the float and clearfix capabilities in an unconstrained parent element. This technique is useful in many applications and becomes a necessary requirement for building well-designed layered architectures.


Description

  1. Refactor the previous lab08a ("Floats") exercise, except remove the height on the <ul> or the <div> (depending on your solution), and instead employ "clearfix" to force the purple background to display. 
  2. For purposes of this exercise, work from the supplied files:lab08b.html and lab08b.css as these are already finished and working from the float exercise.
